# Aether: Problem Statement (One Page)

## Why Aether Exists

Founders and small teams building important digital platforms — especially those serving whānau and communities in Aotearoa — face a difficult reality:

They are expected to move fast while maintaining high standards of quality, accessibility, security, and cultural safety. Most current AI tools increase speed but reduce control, consistency, and sovereignty.

**Aether exists to close this gap.**

---

## The Core Problems

| Problem                        | Impact |
|--------------------------------|--------|
| High cognitive load            | Constant context switching drains founders and slows progress |
| Repetitive technical work      | Many tasks (auth guards, migrations, UI patterns, build fixes) are repeated unnecessarily |
| Inconsistent quality           | AI-generated work often lacks accessibility, security, or cultural alignment |
| Weak oversight                 | Most tools offer little human control over high-impact decisions |
| Lack of sovereignty            | Few tools are designed with Te Mana Raraunga or Māori data sovereignty in mind |
| No compounding intelligence    | Every session starts from scratch with little learning from previous work |

---

## Alignment with NZ Government AI Strategy

Aether is designed to support the principles outlined in **New Zealand’s AI Strategy**, particularly:

- **Human oversight and accountability** — Strong Human-in-the-Loop (HITL) model with clear approval gates.
- **Trustworthy and transparent AI** — Decisions, tool use, and skill applications are logged and reviewable.
- **Māori data sovereignty and Te Tiriti alignment** — Te Mana Raraunga principles are embedded in guardrails, skill design, and system architecture.
- **Inclusive and responsible innovation** — Focus on accessibility (WCAG 2.2 AA), cultural safety, and community benefit.
- **Building domestic capability** — Designed to be sovereign, self-hostable, and usable by New Zealand teams without heavy reliance on foreign cloud infrastructure.

---

## How Aether Solves These Problems

| Approach                        | How It Helps |
|--------------------------------|--------------|
| **Structured Skills**           | Reusable, versioned capabilities that encode standards (UI, security, migrations, etc.) |
| **Tool-Augmented Orchestrator** | Can search code, read files, query memory, and (with approval) write changes |
| **ReAct-Style Reasoning**       | Plans and acts in loops while staying transparent about its decisions |
| **Strong HITL Model**           | Humans stay in control of important actions through clear approval gates |
| **Cultural & Data Sovereignty** | Guardrails and design principles are grounded in Te Mana Raraunga |
| **Persistent Memory**           | Learns from past work to reduce repeated effort over time |

---

## Who It’s For

- Founders and small teams building community-focused platforms
- Projects that require high standards of accessibility, security, and cultural safety
- Teams that want to move faster **without** giving up control or sovereignty

**Primary focus**: The Whānau Preterm Support Hub NZ

---

## Guiding Principles

- User sovereignty first
- Search before building
- Completeness over shortcuts
- Principled self-improvement with care
- Te Mana Raraunga as a core constraint
- Alignment with New Zealand’s AI Strategy
