## Maori / whenua funds - extra gate

Before pursuing funds that require kaitono Maori-led delivery:

1. Confirm real partnership pathway (named iwi/hapu/marae/entity or clear plan to secure one).
2. Require cultural review readiness.
3. Do not invent endorsement or relationship.

## Fit reminder

Use `fit-matrix.md` for numeric scoring. Theme match alone is never enough for R&D funds without technical uncertainty and evidence.
